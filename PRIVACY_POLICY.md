# Privacy Policy for FE-Tools

**Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

## Overview

FE-Tools is a browser extension designed to provide utility tools for front-end developers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how FE-Tools handles your data.

## Data Collection

**FE-Tools does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** Specifically:

- We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
- We do **not** collect browsing history or website content
- We do **not** use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services
- We do **not** send any data to external servers

## Local Storage

FE-Tools uses Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) solely to save your tool preferences, such as:

- Your list of favorite/frequently used tools
- Your currently selected sidebar tool

This data is stored **entirely on your local device** and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

## Data Processing

All data processing in FE-Tools occurs **locally within your browser**. Features such as JSON parsing, color conversion, timestamp conversion, encoding/decoding, and QR code generation are performed entirely on your device. No input data is sent to any external service.

## Permissions

FE-Tools requests the following browser permissions:

- **`storage`**: To save your tool preferences locally on your device
- **`sidePanel`**: To display the tool interface in the browser's side panel

## Third-Party Services

FE-Tools does **not** use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/admin-zlj/fe-tools/issues).
